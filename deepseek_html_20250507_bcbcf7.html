What is Motion?

Motion is when something moves from one place to another. When you run, jump, or throw a ball, you're making motion happen!

What is a Force?

A force is a push or a pull that makes things move or stop moving.

Try this: Push your book across the desk. You used force!

Gravity - The Invisible Force

Gravity is a force that pulls everything down toward Earth. That's why when you jump, you come back down!
